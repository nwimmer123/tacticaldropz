Tactical Dropz and 11th Edition

Hi Tactical Dropz users!

I had a long list of features I wanted to work on, but decided to wait for 11th Edition before making any major changes. After reading the rules and watching some of the early tournament discussions, it looks like there are going to be some significant changes to how missions and terrain are handled.

One thing I'm still trying to figure out is how best to support the new Force Disposition system. Since terrain layouts can potentially change based on the combination of player dispositions, it's not yet clear how tournaments will handle this in practice. My initial thought was to have users select their disposition, their opponent's disposition, and then the resulting terrain layout (A, B, or C). However, since you don't know your opponent's disposition ahead of time, and because reorganizing terrain before every round may be cumbersome, I'm curious to see what actually happens at events.

My suspicion is that many TOs may choose a single terrain layout for an event or round and have all tables use that layout, but I'd love to hear from people who are already playing 11th Edition events.

A few questions:

  • Are any of you TOs?
  • Have any of you played in an 11th Edition tournament yet?
  • How was terrain handled?
  • What do you find most useful about Tactical Dropz today?
  • What features would you most like to see added?

Some features I'm currently considering:

  • 11th Edition map support
    • Option to switch between 10th and 11th Edition layouts
  • Table Setup Mode
    • Display x/y coordinates for terrain piece corners
    • Click any table corner to redefine the coordinate origin
    • Easier recreation of official terrain layouts
  • Base size database
    • Automatically populate base sizes when importing an army list
  • Army themes
    • Add faction artwork and visual customization to the deployment screen

Thanks for using Tactical Dropz, and thanks in advance for any feedback.

– Noah Wimmer
